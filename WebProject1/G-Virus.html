Umbrella Corp.'s Experimental Bio-Weaponry

  • Briefiing: Causes mutations profusely, however,there is no transmittance. Which means the Golgotha-Virus' rate of infection is alomst non-existent.
  • Transmittance: Injection
  • Symptoms: resuscitation of dead cells;muations

  • Effects:Golgotha has extreme effects on the human body. The virus infects cells close by, turning them into so-called "G-Cells". This is seen in Dr. Birkin, who infected himself in the right arm.
